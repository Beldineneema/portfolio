When Trevor was at Maryvale school chapter 6, the school always had Friday mass. The mass
had some rules that Trevor never understood made no sense to him. Things like; standing,
sitting and kneeling on and off and thereafter students who were Catholics could eat the blood
and body of Jesus which was grape juice and crackers but Trevor could not eat because he was
not a Catholic. This act of eating Jesus’ blood and body of Jesus really disturbed Noah,
nevertheless he never got any answer to it. Because he knew Jesus was for everyone and not for
The Catholics alone. So one morning before the mass had started he decided to go against the Catholic rules
.”I’m going to get me some Jesus’ blood and body.” said Trevor to himself. This act was
funny but risky all the same because it could have caused trouble from both the school and
his mum but funnily enough, unexpected happened that his mum sided with him and took him from
that school which was what he wanted, to get out of that school.


Trevor also narrates to us in his book Born A Crime of how he once got arrested by cops
with the accusation of driving a stolen car with a wrong plate. He says that when he arrested
